BARDA VENTURES




BARDA Ventures will work with venture capital partners to invest in promising products and technologies, driving them to the marketplace to transform and enhance national health security.

The BARDA Ventures team have been hard at work over the last year undertaking market research and engaging with the venture capital and investment community. BARDA is pleased to announce the expansion of its market research effort by issuing a Request for Information (RFI) in order to collect feedback on the draft solicitation document. BARDA Ventures is a key component of BARDA’s Medical Countermeasure Innovation Partnership (MCIP) under the 21st Century Cures Act.

BARDA Ventures, through a third-party non-profit, will provide dilutive investment into promising health security products and technologies to enhance national health security preparedness and response. The MCIP initiative is a key priority of ASPR and BARDA, helping to ensure that we fulfil our mission to save lives and protect Americans from 21st century health security threats. BARDA Division of Research Innovation and Ventures (DRIVe) seeks to partner with a third party entity (Ventures Partner) that will address gaps in preparedness and areas within the continuum of response which require innovative and entrepreneurial approaches that would otherwise not be considered under traditional medical countermeasure (“MCM”).

For all those interested parties – BARDA wants to hear from you! We thank those who enthusiastically provided feedback to our earlier RFI and look forward to a similar level of excitement in providing feedback to this draft solicitation. Your participation is invaluable in helping shape the final solicitation. Interested parties must respond to the RFI by November 18, 2019, 09:00am ET.
