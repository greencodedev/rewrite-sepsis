The DRIVe Team

The DRIVe Team of dedicated scientists and entrepreneurs have embraced the challenge of changing how the government approaches the nation’s big health challenges. They work to build an ecosystem of restless innovators who offer solutions to those national health challenges, to health security threats, or to mitigate the damages and loss of life associated with catastrophic events.
